1 - Open a file you want to generate the Table of Content for
2 - Copy and paste the following where you want your Table of Content to appear in the document:
<!-- TOC -->
<!-- /TOC -->
3 - Mark all the entries you want in the Table of Content with '#'. (The number of '#' marks the indentation. Ex: ## -> 1.1.)
4 - Click on 'Tools / Update TOC'
5 - The file is directly updated
6 - Verify that the result is as expected
